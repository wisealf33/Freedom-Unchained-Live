Bridge Bucks signup walkthrough

Join Bridge Bucks Under Freedom Unchained

If you want to join Bridge Bucks yourself, this page walks you through the process clearly. The main thing to get right is the difference between the referral name you create for yourself and the parent affiliate you are joining under.

Before you begin

Start from the Freedom Unchained referral path so you are entering through the correct handoff. Then follow the steps below carefully.

Exact wording to use: in the Parent affiliate field, enter Freedom_Unchained exactly as written.

Step-by-step walkthrough

Step 1

Open the signup path

Use the Bridge Bucks participation link connected to Freedom Unchained. When the page opens, confirm that the URL includes a_aid=Freedom_Unchained.

Step 2

Complete the PMA agreement

Read through the PMA membership agreement, fill in the requested name and email fields, sign it, and submit it to continue.

Step 3

Choose Create New

After the agreement step, select Create New to begin setting up your own Bridge Bucks account.

Step 4

Create your own Referral ID

In the Referral ID field, choose the referral name you want for yourself. This is your own ID, so you can choose what you want as long as it is available and accepted by their system.

Step 5

Enter the correct Parent affiliate

In the Parent affiliate field, type exactly: Freedom_Unchained.

Common mistake: do not put Freedom_Unchained in the Referral ID field unless that is the personal referral name you are creating for yourself. It belongs in the Parent affiliate field so your account is placed correctly under Garrett.

Step 6

Finish the account form

Complete the rest of the signup fields with your own information, review the form, agree to the terms and conditions, and submit the signup.

Quick field summary

  • Referral ID = the referral name you create for yourself
  • Parent affiliate = Freedom_Unchained
