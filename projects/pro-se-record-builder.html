Planning Stage

Pro Se Record Builder

A planning-stage tool for people who need to organize facts, timelines, documents, questions, and due-process issues before trying to speak clearly for themselves.

What it is

A guided record-building system for clearer preparation, better timelines, document organization, and plain-language issue mapping.

Why it matters

People often lose clarity before they ever get to substance. A cleaner record can help people think, prepare, and communicate with more discipline.

What could be built first

  • A timeline builder.
  • A document checklist.
  • A claim/issue mapper.
  • Plain-language due-process education pages.

What kind of help would move it forward

Support from builders, legal-process researchers, writers, template designers, and donors who want to fund the first usable version.

How this connects

This project is one branch of Freedom Unchained. The larger mission is to build practical alternatives rooted in natural law, voluntary cooperation, local strength, and real-world action.

Move this from plan to tool.

Support can fund the first version. Founders Circle is the path for builders who want to help shape it directly.
