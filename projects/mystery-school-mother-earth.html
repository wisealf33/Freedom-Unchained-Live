Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation

A private membership association being developed as an online-first, spiritual-centered community with practical member value, local circles, aligned vendors and practitioners, and real-world Mother Earth Market events.

What it is

Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation is not the umbrella itself. It is one PMA branch that Foundation Freedom can help structure, incubate, and steward into a real membership ecosystem.

Current planning focus

The current work is to define the PMA front door, member entry path, vendor and practitioner upgrade model, local-circle pathway, event model, newsletter, directory, and first-step launch plan.

Core structure

The launch model starts with a simple PMA front door. People enter as Basic Members, with deeper opportunities opening inside the membership container.

  • Basic Member entry point at $33 per year
  • Vendor / Practitioner Member Upgrade as the first major upgrade path
  • Member opportunities framed inside the PMA, not as public direct-sale services

Ecosystem pieces

The project is designed as a connected umbrella for online community, local chapters, promotion, and gathering.

  • Online Mystery School community
  • Mother Earth Circles as local chapters
  • Mother Earth Market as a local vendor and community event model
  • Newsletter, directory, and promotional placements for members

Launch path

The online ecosystem is the now-stage product and audience engine. The local work grows from that foundation instead of waiting for a physical event to prove the concept first.

  • Build the PMA umbrella and public invitation
  • Attract members, vendors, practitioners, and supporters online
  • Promote aligned people through newsletter, directory, and community channels
  • Use that momentum to form the first local circle around Peotone / South Chicagoland

Mother Earth Market

The first practical event model gathers local vendors, growers, makers, healers, teachers, green businesses, and community members in real life.

  • Possible pilot entry: $5
  • Guests receive five $1 Spirit Bucks
  • Spirit Bucks can encourage multiple vendor purchases while vendors keep their sales
  • Rules and PMA/event wording still need careful review before public launch
How it relates to Foundation Freedom

Foundation Freedom is the incubator and institutional-building lane. Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation is one specific PMA being built under that umbrella.
