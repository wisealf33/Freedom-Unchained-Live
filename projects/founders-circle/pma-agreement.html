Founders' Circle Private Membership Association Agreement

By signing this agreement, I request to participate as a private member of Founders' Circle, a private working circle formed for mutual support, project development, education, voluntary association, and principled collaboration.

I understand that Founders' Circle is not a casual spectator community. It is intended for serious participants who are willing to work on their own projects while also supporting other members and their projects.

I agree to act with honesty, discretion, personal responsibility, and respect for the private nature of member conversations, shared ideas, documents, and project discussions.

I understand that membership may be accepted, paused, or ended by the association if my participation is no longer aligned with the purpose, standards, or working culture of the circle.

I understand that payment alone does not create membership unless acceptance has been granted and this agreement has been completed.
