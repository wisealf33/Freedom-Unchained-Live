Aligned Resources

This page is for organizations, projects, communities, and tools that are not formally affiliated with me, but may still be helpful to people exploring the same broader direction, stronger communities, parallel infrastructure, voluntary cooperation, decentralization, self-governance, and practical alternatives.

How to read this page

This is not meant to blur lines, imply partnership, or present outside groups as official extensions of Freedom Unchained. It is simply a place to collect useful, values-adjacent referrals that may help support the larger ecosystem this mission points toward.

  • Some may be strong value alignment.
  • Some may be practical tools or useful infrastructure.
  • Some may simply be worth exploring as neighboring efforts.

Starter categories

Parallel institutions

Organizations building alternative structures

Groups working on private membership, decentralized organization, alternative governance, arbitration, mutual aid, or community-first institutional forms.

This category matters because real alternatives need more than ideas. They need structures people can actually gather around and use.

Community support

Networks helping people connect and organize

Communities, circles, and local-first networks that help people find one another, share skills, trade value, or build healthier support systems outside heavily centralized channels.

This category matters because strong communities are one of the foundations of any freer future.

Tools and platforms

Useful infrastructure for coordination and independence

Tools that may help with communication, coordination, exchange, sovereignty, privacy, publishing, or decentralized operation.

This category matters because practical infrastructure can lower the barrier to building something real.

Neighboring missions

Projects moving in a similar direction

Other efforts that may not match this mission exactly, but still reflect serious work toward freedom, local strength, voluntary cooperation, resilience, or parallel systems.

This category matters because useful overlap can exist without formal alignment.

What comes next

Right now this page is a clean placeholder and collection point. Over time, it can grow into a curated list of real referrals, organizations, and tools, each with a short note explaining why it may be relevant.

Stay connected to the larger mission

Freedom Unchained is still the main front door. This page simply widens the map so people can find useful neighboring efforts without confusing them for direct affiliates.
