Build your idea / PMA pathway

Your Vision

If you have an idea for the new free world, this is the invitation to begin shaping it with structure, trust, and aligned people. Your project may become its own private membership association, branch, community, tool, gathering, or practical offering under the larger mission.

Bring the idea out of your head

Many people carry a project, calling, service, or community vision that never becomes real because it has no container. This page is for turning that spark into a clearer path.

Build with the right container

Some ideas need more than a public page or casual group. They may need a PMA-style structure, a trusted circle, clear expectations, and a way for people to participate with shared purpose.

What kind of vision fits here?

This is for ideas that help build a freer, healthier, more human world. That could include local resilience, education, aligned community, health and wellness, food systems, family support, media, markets, technology, or other parallel structures.

  • A project you want to build but do not want to build alone.
  • A service or community that may need a private membership association framework.
  • A practical tool, circle, market, school, gathering, or local initiative.
  • A serious idea that needs refinement, accountability, and aligned collaborators.

The first step: Founders Circle

Founders Circle can become the front door for this process. Instead of asking people to launch a whole PMA or project alone, the first step is to request to join a private working circle where the idea can be tested, clarified, and connected to the right next move.

1

Request to join Founders Circle

Start by stepping into the circle of builders, thinkers, organizers, and serious collaborators already gathering around this mission.

2

Share the vision clearly

Bring the idea, the people it serves, the problem it answers, and what kind of support or structure it may need.

3

Shape the container

Together, explore whether the idea belongs as a PMA, project branch, local circle, resource, public page, event, or private working group.

4

Move toward a first version

The goal is not endless discussion. The goal is a first useful version, a clear invitation, and a path that can actually be walked.

Start with the circle

If you have a real idea and want to explore whether it belongs inside this larger build, request to join Founders Circle and begin the conversation there.
