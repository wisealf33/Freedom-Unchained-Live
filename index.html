Who I Am

I’m Garrett J Grubbs, the man behind Freedom Unchained. I’m focused on bringing together ideas, people, and projects that can help build a more free, decentralized, and community-driven future through real work, real participation, and practical action.

Purpose

The purpose of this work is to help redirect our energy away from endless attention on broken systems and toward building something better. Not just criticizing what is failing, but creating parallel structures people can actually rely on, structures rooted in freedom, free will, self-governance, voluntary cooperation, and stronger local communities.

Mission

My mission is to help build real-world alternatives that can take the place of centralized systems without losing the functions people still need. That means building toward stronger communities, decentralized collaboration, parallel economies, fair arbitration and dispute resolution, community-based security and support systems, better coordination between people and places, and new ways for communities to work together, build infrastructure, solve problems, and create value without depending on coercive control.

Call to Action

This is for builders, creators, organizers, contributors, problem-solvers, and people who know we need something more. If you are here to connect, collaborate, contribute skills, share ideas, follow the work, explore the projects, or help build what comes next, you are in the right place. And if you are here first to watch and learn, you are welcome too, follow along, share what matters, and step in when you are ready.

Projects Connected to Freedom Unchained

Localized.life
